Austin Social Gaming Community

Diverse gaming community that hosts regular game nights at Emerald Tavern and Bat City Games & Comics. They also organize events through their Discord server. Great for both new and experienced gamers looking to make friends and play games.

g3 Games

Inclusive gaming community hosting weekly events at Kerbey Lane and online. Features board games, card games, and tabletop RPGs. Nonprofit organization focused on building community through gaming.

Boards and Brews

Active board gaming group that meets 3-4 times a month at The Brewtorium. They play a variety of games from classics to Euro games, with a focus on social gaming and craft beer. Regular open gaming nights on Tuesdays.

Lady+ Geeks of Austin

Active group for women-identifying and non-binary geeks in Austin and surrounding areas. Founded in 2016, they host various events including board game nights, themed parties, book discussions, craft events, and more.

Hub for Austin TTRPG community connecting GMs and players. Hosts multi-table D&D events, open games, and playtests. Also supports LARPs, meetups, and GM workshops.
