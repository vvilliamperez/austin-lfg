Boards and Brews

Active board gaming group that meets 3-4 times a month at The Brewtorium. They play a variety of games from classics to Euro games, with a focus on social gaming and craft beer. Regular open gaming nights on Tuesdays.

Meetup

Games for Connection

Weekly card and board game meetup group that meets every Thursday at Central Market Cafe. They focus on social interaction and connection through various card and relationship games. Regular meetups from 6:00 PM to 9:00 PM.

Meetup

Austin Social Gaming Community

Diverse gaming community that hosts regular game nights at Bat City Games & Comics on alternating Saturdays (6-10 PM). They also organize events through their Discord server. Great for both new and experienced gamers looking to make friends and play games.

Meetup Discord

South Austin - Games & Grub

Weekly board game meetup at Pinthouse Brewing every Wednesday (6:30 PM). Focuses on medium-weight strategy games like Scythe, Concordia, and Wingspan. Great for both new and experienced gamers, with friendly teaching and a welcoming environment.
